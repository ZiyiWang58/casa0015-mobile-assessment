## Introduction to Application

**PawTrack** is a mobile application designed to help busy dog owners manage their dogs’ daily walking routines more clearly and consistently. The app was developed in Flutter and focuses on a simple but practical problem: owners may forget to walk their dogs, do not remember how far they walked, or whether the dog’s exercise target has been reached.

The application allows users to create dog profiles, set walking goals, and track walks in real time using GPS. During a walk, PawTrack records route, distance, time, and calories, then presents a summary when the walk ends. The app also includes history and calendar views so users can review previous walks and monitor routine over time.

To make the app more useful in real situations, PawTrack integrates live weather information and turns it into simple walking advice. It also uses Firebase Authentication and Cloud Firestore so users can register, log in, and keep their dog profiles and walk records stored separately under their own accounts.

Overall, PawTrack combines dog management, activity tracking, reminders, and cloud-based user data into one connected mobile experience.
